### Introduction
This project is a personal portfolio website for Isabelle Santos de Jesus, a graphic designer and digital printing specialist. It serves as an online showcase of her professional work, skills, and experience in the field of graphic design, illustration, and print production. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages under the repository `stygian-blue.github.io`, providing a static, responsive platform to highlight her expertise and attract potential clients or employers.

### About
Isabelle Santos de Jesus is a graphic designer with a background in Visual Arts from UNOESTE. She specializes in digital printing, pre-press operations, and illustration, with hands-on experience in offset plate engraving, file preparation, and creating visual materials like cards, flyers, banners, and catalogs. Her work combines creativity with technical precision, and she has held roles at Gráfica Impress (as a junior plate engraver and designer) and Lebazi Indústria e Comércio (as an administrative assistant and designer). She also taught drawing at EBAC, enhancing her communication and teaching skills. Isabelle is proficient in English (intermediate) and Japanese (basic), and seeks opportunities in graphic design, pre-press, and digital printing where she can contribute her aesthetic vision and technical knowledge.

### Technologies Used
- **HTML5**: For structuring the website's content, including sections for hero, experience, skills, and projects.
- **CSS3**: For styling, including custom CSS variables for a cohesive color scheme (e.g., blues and beiges), responsive design with media queries, and effects like backdrop blur and hover animations.
- **JavaScript (Vanilla)**: For dynamic functionality, such as populating the projects grid, handling modal overlays for project details, and implementing a lightbox for image/video galleries.
- **Google Fonts (Montserrat)**: For typography, providing a clean, modern font family.
- **GitHub Pages**: For hosting the static site directly from the repository.
- **Image Hosting**: External services like GitHub raw links and Cloudinary for project images and videos.
- **Design Tools (Referenced in Skills)**: CorelDRAW, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, CapCut, Figma, and Microsoft Office suite, though these are tools Isabelle uses professionally rather than technologies powering the site itself.
